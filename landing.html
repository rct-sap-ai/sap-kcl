The Challenge

SAPs are essential,
but writing them is slow

Statistical analysis plans are central to the credibility of randomised clinical trials, yet they remain cognitively demanding and time consuming to produce.

⚠

Resource Intensive

SAPs must be written prior to unblinded data review, typically before the first Data Monitoring Committee meeting.

✓

Critical for Credibility

They eliminate discretionary analytic decisions, provide a specification for coding, and support transparent reporting.

⚙

Highly Structured

JAMA guidance outlines requirements covering analysis populations, model specifications, multiplicity, and sensitivity analyses.

★

AI Opportunity

LLMs accelerate drafting of descriptive sections, freeing statisticians for complex methodological decisions.

How It Works

Protocol to code
in three steps

SAPAI walks you through a structured workflow, using AI to extract trial details and generate both documentation and executable analysis scripts.

1

Upload Protocol

Upload your trial protocol as PDF, DOCX, or text. AI extracts design, endpoints, randomisation, and sample size.

2

Define & Refine

Review and edit AI suggested timepoints, outcome measures, and statistical analyses. You stay in control at every step.

3

Generate Outputs

Download a draft SAP as Word and a ZIP with executable R or Python analysis code, ready for your trial.
